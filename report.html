    View Lab Reports


    This is a secure and convenient tool that allows you to view your lab reports online .

    Please read the following instructions before contacting the support help line :

    1. Please enter your invoice number and access code to view your lab reports (view sample invoice with access code).
    2. Only Pathology reports are accessible online. Any other reports (ECG, CT, X-Ray, MRI, etc.) are not available online. For radiology reports, kindly visit our Hospitals (in Lahore or Peshawar) or our Diagnostic Centres (in Lahore or Karachi).
    3. Reports can be viewed for a period of 6 months from invoice date.
    4. If you are accessing this website from behind a firewall, your server's firewall security might not allow you to open this page. In this case, please contact your network administrator for further assistance.
    5. Please download and print your own reports. Support staff cannot email the reports.
    6. In case of any assistance, please feel free to contact the Pathology Support Help Line at 042 3590 5133.

    See Direction maps to SKMCH&RC, Lahore and SKMCH, Peshawar .
