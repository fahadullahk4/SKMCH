    Facts and Statistics

    View our fact-sheets to learn about the Hospital facts and the latest financial and patient statistics.

    Inaugurated        December 29, 1994

    Founder        Imran Khan (Chairman, Board of Governors)

    Annual budget        Rs. 10 billion (Year 2017)

    Financially supported patients        75% (since 1994)

    Philanthropic spending to date        Rs. 27.240 billion (US$ 319 Million)

    Area/Location        20 acres in Lahore, Pakistan

    Beds        195

    New registrations        10,203 (Year 2016)

    Outpatient visits        236,351 (Year 2016)

    Chemotherapy        52,843 (Year 2016)

    Radiation treatments        65,759 (Year 2016)

    Admissions        12,576 (Year 2016)

    Surgical procedures        13,817 (Year 2016)

    Pathology tests        5,015,237 (Year 2016)

    Imaging studies        174,932 (Year 2016)

    Pharmacy Dispensing        1,578,100 (Year 2016)

    Staff        2,458
