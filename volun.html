    Volunteer

    Thank you for your interest in volunteering at Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital and Research Centre. Volunteers register to assist or undertake activities at the Hospital of their
    own free will and without remuneration.

    Criteria for Applicants:
    Applicants must have a minimum of matriculation or equivalent qualification. Volunteers are selected keeping
    in view the applicant's interests & commitments and the volunteering opportunities we offer.

    • Volunteers who have served a minimum period of two weeks are awarded an appreciation certificate in recognition of their services.

    Apply Online

    For any specific queries , please contact :
