    Getting an Appointment


    Appointments with any of our physicians may be obtained by calling the Hospital at +92 42 35905000 Ext. 3433 or 3435, or by emailing the OPD Manager at outpatient@skm.org.pk . Our outpatient clinics function from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday-Friday but please note that a particular doctor may not be available at all times. If you are making a request via email, please provide the following :-

    • A phone number where you can be reached.
    • A concise description of your problem to allow scheduling with the most relevant specialty
      or the name of the physician with whom an appointment is desired.
    • Scanned copies of the most relevant tests reports with your email.
    • Any preference about date and time
    Onine Consultations

    SKMCH&RC physicians do not provide online consultations to patients in view of the inherent danger involved in providing advice based only on reports and without the benefit of examining a patient.
