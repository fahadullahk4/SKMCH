    Education and Training

    The mission of SKMCH&RC is to act as a model institution to alleviate the suffering of patients with cancer through the application of modern methods of curative and palliative therapy
    irrespective of their ability to pay, the education of health care professionals and the public, and perform research into the causes and treatment of cancer.
