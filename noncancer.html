    Non-Cancer Services


    The Shaukat Khanum Memorial Trust offers state-of-the-art diagnostic and clinical services with
    the help of latest healthcare technology and a team of highly skilled and trained healthcare
    staff.

    In addition to Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital and Research Centres in Lahore and Peshawar,
    Shaukat Khanum Walk-in Clinics, and Shaukat Khanum Diagnostic Centres, the Shaukat Khanum
    Memorial Trust offers a wide range of cancer and non-cancer diagnostic services through its network
    of Laboratory Collection Centres present all across Pakistan.

      Shaukat Khanum Pathology Laboratory, one of the nation's largest and most sophisticated facilities, uses state-of-the-art equipment for all tests, setting a standard for result accuracy and reliability.
